What types of vehicles does Luxury Rent a Car offer?

What are the rental requirements?
Generally, renters must be at least 25 years old (exceptions may apply), possess a valid driver's license, and provide a major credit card for payment and security deposit purposes.
Can I rent a car for a special event or long-term rental?
Yes, Luxury Rent a Car offers special event rentals and long-term rental options tailored to your specific needs. Contact us for more information and personalized assistance.
How can I modify or cancel my reservation?
You can modify or cancel your reservation by contacting our customer service team directly. Please review our cancellation policy for any applicable fees or deadlines.
What happens if I return the car late?
